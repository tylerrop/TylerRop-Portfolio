Vietnam's past reads like a romantic legend, full of heroic battles and impressive victories. This small country has managed to preserve its culture, absorbing and adapting to the strengths of its opponents, without losing its traditions or its sense of history. With 54 ethnic groups most of whom live in remote areas and follow old customs, Vietnam offers unparalleled opportunities for cultural discoveries.

Experience daily life in the Mekong Delta, Vietnam’s busiest agricultural hub


Discovery of the ancient town of Hoi An, a UNESCO world heritage site
