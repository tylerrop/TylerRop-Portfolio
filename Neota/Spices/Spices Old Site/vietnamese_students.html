Canadian University Tour for Vietnamese and International Students -
July/August 2009

If you are a high school student in Vietnam, 16 years or older, we invite you to participate in a tour of Canadian Universities in July and August of 2009. This tour will help you discover the essence of University life in Canada. The tour will feature different Canadian Universities, and will provide a great opportunity for students to have a taste of daily life in the English speaking context of North America all the while having the option to travel through the dynamic cities of Canada from the Pacific to the Atlantic Oceans. After visiting select campuses, you will then participate in an English Language Immersion Program at a well recognized Canadian University.

Why is this a great opportunity for you?

Only with SPICES Study tours will you discover Canadian Universities and Colleges in this unique and engaging way. In each city you will be greeted by University staff, provided with a campus tour, and experience residence life while taking in the atmosphere and sights of each distinct institution. Social activities will include various experiences such as sporting events, barbeques and cultural festivals. You will be actively engaged and immersed in the English Language all the while traveling across Canada and making new friends!

Summer English Language Program

McMaster University is situated in Hamilton, Ontario, just a short drive from Toronto and the Niagara Falls region. This four week intensive English Language Study Program will include 30 hours of English classes each week with qualified and experienced teachers. The instruction will include15 hours of in class study and 15 hours of practical work.

Explore the opportunities of studying in Canada! Our team of experts are uniquely qualified to help you go all the way!
