About Us

At Stemz Florist, our aim is to offer stunning flowers and plants at an affordable price. We regularly stock our coolers with top-quality products and create beautiful arrangements to suit any occasion. Shop through our amazing selection and find the perfect flowers and gifts for your loved ones.


We offer a wide range of flowers and floral gifts, from hand tied bouquets to plants and tributes, servicing customers throughout the Calgary area. We combine contemporary and traditional floristry with great service and a wide choice of only the freshest flowers to create innovative and inspirational design. We guarantee to deliver quality leaving you and your recipients delighted, satisfied, and amazed. We custom-design for all budgets and offer consultations for special events. As well, Stemz Florist & Treasures stocks unique "treasures" - in limited quantities - guaranteed for that "one of a kind" gift!"
