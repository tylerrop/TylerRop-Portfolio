the team
SPICES Study Tours was the brainchild of two Canadian educators, Claudette Croteau and Dan Ton-That. The original idea of study tours has been enlarged with an insight into all economic and business apects of life, to encompass all forms of human development urgently needed in modern open societies. Knowledge of historical and cultural backgrounds is especially useful for people from economically advanced countries who wish to conduct business with emerging countries. The reverse is equally true. All people living in advanced economies as well in emerging economies should share human knowledge, culture and experiences. Both educators have been working together trying to organize all form of cross-cultural exchanges and training urgently needed in emerging countries with the help of well renowned learning institutions in North America and highly qualified professionals in their respective fields of endeavor.
