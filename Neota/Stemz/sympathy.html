Sympathy

Stemz Florist & Treasures understands how difficult it is to lose a loved one. Whether you are sending sympathy flowers on behalf of yourself, your family, a group of friends, or a business, we'll take care of it for you. Sending a sympathy floral arrangement be made easily with the help of our friendly staff. We have years of experience in creating thoughtful and beautiful tributes to great people.

Casket sprays, floral wreaths and funeral baskets are also areas to which we take great pride. We will take care to make sure that your floral tributes arrive at the Funeral Home for prayers and/or a viewing when requested. Often a cheerful bouquet sent to the family is appreciate the days after the service.

To schedule assistance, please contact us at (403) 242-6690 or drop by the shop. We will be happy to discuss your requests.
