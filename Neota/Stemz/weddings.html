Weddings

So you're getting married... Congratulations!
In order to help make the flowers the least of your worries, follow this Wedding Flower Check list:

Six Months Before the Wedding: Choose your florist. Note that a deposit will be requested to reserve your wedding date. At Stemz Florist and Treasures we ask for a 10% deposit.

Two Months Before the Wedding: Meet with the florist to decide on the flowers. Bring in fabric samples of your bridesmaids gown and any pictures that have appealed to you. Also, keep in mind that the latest fashionable color may not suit you. Be practical. The designers at Stemz Florist & Treasures will be happy to discuss the details as well as the fashions.

Establish a Budget for your Wedding Flowers. This will make the decision- making process less confusing. When placing a wedding order at a busy holiday time, please keep in mind the cost of flowers will go up due to demand. Also, keep in mind that flowers in-season can also save you money.

At Stemz Florist & Treasures, we invite the bride to view our work and ask that you come in the afternoon before your wedding and see the arrangements that have been created just for you. Final payment is due at this time.
